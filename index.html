About Me

I consider myself and simple man with simple tastes. As long as I get to drink some Hot-Chocolate with hazelnut and carmel syrup and wip creme on top and read some manga then I am in bliss. I have loved reading since I was little, especially comic books, it wasnt until I was in middle-shool and high-school that I really discovered the japanese equivilent to comic books, manga. I have also been drawing since I was young and I would draw what I read or saw on that page. This grew into my wanting to learn about how to create my own comics, and that lead me to learning about how to draw and paint digitally instead of physically with real supplies. And THAT lead me all the way into computers and building them and learning how to code. It has been a very DEEP rabbit hole but I can say that it has been an amazing ride!
